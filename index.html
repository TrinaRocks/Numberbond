ℹ️ Teacher / parent notes ▾

About these games

All three games practise number bonds using different representations. Bond selection is fully random from the chosen level — designed for open exploration and iPad concept testing.

Star ratings (recommended stage)

★☆☆ Weighing Scales — best for early KS1, bonds to 10.
★★☆ Ocean Bonds & Potion Shop — KS1 through lower KS2.
★★★ (coming soon) — upper KS2 challenge.

Level & mode settings

Each game has its own settings screen. Choose the bond range (to 5, to 10, to 20, doubles only, etc.) and question type (addition only, subtraction only, or mixed).

Profiles & privacy

Named profiles save the child's last-used level and game so they pick up where they left off. Guest mode stores nothing at all. All data stays on this device — nothing is sent to any server.
